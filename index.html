About G.A. Consultants Ltd

Established in 2003, G.A. Consultants Ltd is a Kenyan engineering consultancy firm providing professional and innovative solutions in civil, structural, transportation, and water engineering.

We are committed to promoting creativity, sustainability and productivity to our clients and the society at large.

Our Vision

To be the preferred engineering consultancy firm that innovates and productively is customer and society focused.

Our Mission

To provide revolutionary and productive engineering services that profitably solves our client's visions.

Sustainability

We integrate sustainable practices in all our projects to better our environment and community.

Innovation

We embrace innovative technologies to deliver efficient, cost-effective and timely solutions.
