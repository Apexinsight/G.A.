Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a project quote?

Simply contact us through the form above or call us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your project requirements and provide a detailed quotation within 5-7 business days.

What is your project delivery timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. We ensure realistic schedules during the planning phase and maintain 95% on-time delivery rate. We'll provide a detailed project schedule upfront.

Do you work with international standards?

Yes, all our projects comply with international engineering standards including ISO, EN, ASTM, and other applicable codes. We ensure global quality standards in every deliverable.

Can you handle emergency projects?

Yes, we can accommodate urgent projects. Contact us directly to discuss your emergency requirements and we'll mobilize resources immediately.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment terms including milestone-based payments, monthly retainers, and project-based pricing. We'll discuss the best option for your project during the initial consultation.

How do you ensure project confidentiality?

Confidentiality is paramount. All project information is protected under strict NDAs and our robust data security protocols ensure complete confidentiality of client information.
