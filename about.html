About G.A. Consultants Ltd

Who We Are and What Drives Us

Company Overview

Established in 2003, G.A. Consultants Ltd is a Kenyan engineering consultancy firm providing professional and innovative solutions in civil, structural, transportation, and water engineering. With over two decades of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering excellence and fostering long-term relationships with our clients.

Our team comprises experienced and professional engineers who are passionate about delivering outstanding results. We take pride in our ability to provide innovative and cost-effective solutions that meet and exceed client expectations.

Core Values

Vision

To be the preferred engineering consultancy firm that innovates and productively is customer and society focused, delivering sustainable solutions that transform infrastructure and communities.

Mission

To provide revolutionary and productive engineering services that profitably solve our client's visions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, Sustainability, Teamwork, and Client-Centricity are the pillars that guide every decision and action we take.
